An integrated cancer drug sensitivity database for in vitro pharmacogenomics analysis

CREAMMIST enables users to obtain an integrated dose-response across five widely used cancer cell-line drug-response datasets. We utilized the Bayesian framework to systematically integrate all available dose-response values across datasets. The integration at the dose-response level provides an extended view of the dose-response curve to users, especially when different ranges of drug dosages were used across experiments. Users can utilize easy-to-use statistics derived from integrative dose-response curves for various downstream analyses (such as identifying biomarkers, selecting dose range for their experiment, and training a machine learning model).
